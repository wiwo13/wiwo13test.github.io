One Piece Live Action 2023

"One Piece: The Grand Voyage" is a thrilling live-action adaptation of the beloved manga and anime series, capturing the essence of the original while delivering a cinematic experience filled with action, humor, and heart. It leaves the door open for future adventures and the continuation of the epic tale."

Little Women 2020

"Little Women" is a poignant and beautifully crafted adaptation of Louisa May Alcott's timeless novel.Express the classic story, offering a fresh perspective coming-of-age journeys, weaves together themes of love, ambition ,a heartwarming and thought-provoking exploration of sisterhood and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Barbie 2023

"Babie" (2023) is a delightful family film that will charm both kids and adults. With its endearing characters, colorful animation, and a heartwarming storyline, it's a joyful journey that celebrates the wonder of childhood. "Babie" delivers humor and life lessons in equal measure, making it a perfect choice for a fun family movie night.

To All the Boys I loved Before 2018

"To All the Boys I've Loved Before" is a sweet and relatable teen romance that hits all the right notes. With its charismatic leads, heartfelt storytelling, and a dash of coming-of-age nostalgia, it's a delightful exploration of love letters, secrets, and the rollercoaster ride of high school emotions.

The Boy in The Striped Pajamas 2007

"The Boy in the Striped Pajamas" is a deeply moving and thought-provoking film that explores the innocence of childhood in the most harrowing of circumstances. Set against the backdrop of the Holocaust, it tells a tragic tale of friendship and the consequences of blind hatred.

The Princess Diaries 2001

"Princess Diaries" is a charming and whimsical coming-of-age film that's both heartwarming and humorous. The movie offers a delightful blend of self-discovery, romance ,and a touch of fairy-tale magic. It's a feel-good classic that appeals to audiences of all ages.
